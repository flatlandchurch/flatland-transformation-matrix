Character

Love

I find it easy to be very understanding of people who criticize me and fail to live up to the standards I hold for them.

Joy

I find pleasure in most everything I do and I am content with all that God has given to me.

Peace

It is easy for me to forgive myself and others, and I am free from stress, anxiety, depression and fear.

Patience

I am quite capable of handling periods of suffering and pressure without becoming irritable or frustrated.

Kindness

I am quick to lend a hand when someone is in need or in trouble, expecting nothing in return.

Faithfulness

I maintaisn my commitments to and think highly of those in authority over me and those who depend on me.

Gentleness

I allow others to make mistakes without becoming withdrawn or angry when it is necessary to confront them.

Self-Control

I manage my time and finances well without the interference of addictive or immoral behavior.

Marital Situation

I am very content with my current marital situation, and I do not wrestle with any type of sexual immorality.

Immediate Family

I have excellent relationships with everyone in my immediate family and communicate with them regularly.

Finances

I view my finances and possessions as blessings from God, giving generously and spending wisely with no indebtedness.

Free-Time

I regularly take time for myself, engaging in activities that strengthen and refresh me rather than trying to escape.

Spiritual Formation

I actively welcome God into my home, set a godly example and encourage the faith of my family members.

Personal Development

I strive to learn new things and deepen my current pool of knowledge, encouraging my family to do the same.

Health

I have a healthy lifestyle in regard to diet, excercise, grooming, and cleanliness without and excessive preoccupation with them.

Hospitality

I welcome people to my home, using opportunity to strengthen and encourage them in life's journey.

Social Network

I have a solid group of friends who keep me accountable, encourage me and strengthen my relationship with God.

Relatives

All my relationships with my extended family and in-laws are vibrant and healthy. I enjoy spending time with them.

Tolerance

I have strong relationships with people who are quite different than me, and I do not wrestle with bias or bigotry of any kind.

Being Mentored

There are several people more experienced than me who personally influence my life on a regular basis.

Mentoring Others

I have a positive, personal influence on the lives of several people with less experience than me.

Compassion

I am generous with and quick to respond to the needs of my friends and family without expecting anything in return.

Receiving Criticism

I like to be challenged by my peers and am willing to accept criticism without lashing out or harboring bitterness.

Forgiveness

When I am hurt or offended, I am quick to confront the responsible person in love and do not speak ill of them to others.

Education

I am well-educated with regard to my work and I am continually learning and applying new information as it develops.

Gifts Match

My work is perfect match with my areas of giftedness. What I do for a living come rather natural for me.

Contribution

I thoroughly enjoy serving the people who benefit from the results of my labor. My work is a tremendous blessing to others.

Employee Relations

I have terrific relationships with everyone I work with regardless of their position. We are a team.

Empowerment

Whenever things are not going right, I am able to influence positive change without offending or ostracizing others.

Goals

I am motivated by compelling, realistic goals that stretch my abilities and challenge me to work with excellence.

Time Management

I work with diligence, completing my tasks in a timely manner, yet leaving sufficient time for other priorities in life.

Enjoyment

My work brings me satisfaction and enjoyment. I can not think of anything else I would rather be doing.

Respect

I am respected and well-liked by people in the community who know me. They like to spend time with me.

Neighbors

I know my immediate neighbors by name and have a good relationship with them. We enjoy living on the same street.

Civic Duties

I gladly fulfill my civic duties (i.e. voting, jury duty, paying taxes, school involvement) when I am called upon to do so.

Peacemaker

I am a peacemaker. I do not engage in conversation or behavior that denigrate people of other races, religions or cultures.

Charitable

When I see an individual or family in need, I am quick to give of my time and my resources to bless them.

Law Abiding

I am an exemplary citizen, refusing to engage in any kind of behavior that could get me in trouble with the law.

Positive Influence

I associate with several groups of people in the community and have a strong, positive influence in their lives.

Cultural Awareness

I spend time with diverse groups of people and have a good understanding of their various cultures and philosophies.

Discovery

I know what my various gifts and talents are and I am often encouraged by others to use them.

Stewardship

I freely use my talents and abilities for the benefit of others without expecting anything in return.

Practice

I regularly spend time sharpening my abilities and strengthening my skills so that I might perform with excellence.

Teachable

I continually seek out others who are more proficient than I am in order to learn from them and improve my talens.

Teaching Others

I can often be found helping others develop their gifts and talents and encouraging them to use them.

Cooperative

Whenever I use my talents and abilities in conjunction with other people, I am a team player. I am often asked back.

Humble

I am not overly concerned with receiving credit for my contributions, and I am content when others surpass my abilities.

Confident

When called upon to use my talents, I am confident that I can get the job done. I enjoy a good challenge.
